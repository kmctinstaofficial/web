KMCT Medical College

KMCT Medical College was established in the year 2008 with an intake of 100 MBBS seats approved by the Medical Council of India. The College is affiliated with Kerala University of Health Sciences. The Central Government had accorded recognition for the MBBS course in the year 2013. Postgraduate courses were started in the academic year 2015-16 in Orthopaedics & Pharmacology with an annual intake of one and two respectively. From the academic year 2016-17, the intake of students in MBBS course was increased from 100 to 150 by the approval of Central Government.

KMCT Dental College

The inception of KMCT Dental College in 2006 was another successful accomplishment of the KMCT Group of Institutions. It is located at Mukkam, about 35 km north-east of Calicut city, Kerala, along the valley of the Western Ghats, India. A town bathed in serenity, greenery and calm, placid rivers. The college is recognized by the Dental Council of India and Government of India. It is affiliated to the Kerala University of Health Sciences (KUHS) from 2010. KMCT Dental College is a minority status institution with a mission of serving humanity inculcating supreme moral values to the students.
